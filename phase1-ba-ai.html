What is this?
This is Phase 1 of a structured training roadmap designed to help business analysts transition into AI project roles. It builds the foundational AI literacy you need to contribute meaningfully to AI initiatives — without requiring you to write code.
Who it's for
Business analysts experienced with Power BI, Tableau, and stakeholder management who want to understand AI well enough to gather requirements, assess feasibility, and bridge the gap between technical AI teams and business stakeholders.
Duration & Approach
Approximately 3 months at 1–2 hours per day on weekdays. Focus on starred (⭐) courses first. You don't need to finish every course — use them as references and move on once you grasp the core concepts.
🎯
Phase 1 Goal
Be able to confidently explain what AI, ML, deep learning, and generative AI are. Understand how AI models are trained, what data they need, and what their limitations are. Use AI tools like ChatGPT effectively in your daily BA work. Create a one-page AI opportunity assessment for a real business process you know well.
⏱️
