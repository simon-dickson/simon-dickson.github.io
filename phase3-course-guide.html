About Phase 3
Phase 3 is the most technically demanding phase. Deep learning requires genuine patience — the concepts are hard at first. Focus on building intuition before chasing mathematical perfection.
What You'll Build
By the end you should be comfortable training neural networks, working with PyTorch, and using HuggingFace to fine-tune pre-trained models — the skills needed for most AI Engineer job descriptions.
Framework Strategy
Don't learn PyTorch AND TensorFlow simultaneously. Start with PyTorch (Section 2), build your milestone project in PyTorch, then learn TensorFlow/Keras as a secondary skill alongside the Neural Networks Bootcamp theory.
🎯
Phase 3 Milestone Project
Fine-tune a pre-trained HuggingFace model on a classification or NLP task of your own choosing. Cover: dataset prep → tokenisation → model selection → training → evaluation → FastAPI deployment. Push to GitHub. This project directly demonstrates the skills needed for most AI Engineer job descriptions.
💡
Study Tip
The Neural Networks Bootcamp (Section 1) is your theoretical backbone — read it alongside practical coding, not before. PyTorch is your primary framework; TensorFlow is the secondary skill. Building from scratch (Section 1 Option 2) gives a far deeper understanding of what frameworks actually do.
