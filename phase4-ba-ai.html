What is this?
Phase 4 is where everything comes together. You'll learn to lead AI adoption, manage organisational change, build business cases for AI initiatives, and communicate AI strategy to executives and stakeholders — the capstone skills that make BAs indispensable on AI projects.
The BA advantage
Most AI projects fail not because of technology but because of inadequate change management and stakeholder alignment. Your BA experience in facilitating workshops, managing expectations, and bridging technical and business teams is exactly what AI projects need most.
Duration & Approach
Approximately 3 months. This phase is the most action-oriented — pair the courses with real-world practice. Volunteer for an AI initiative at work, lead a lunch-and-learn, or build a business case for an AI opportunity you've identified.
🎯
Phase 4 Goal
Develop and present an AI business case to leadership. Include: opportunity assessment, feasibility analysis, data readiness review, risk and ethics considerations, change management plan, success metrics, and a phased implementation roadmap. This is your portfolio piece that demonstrates the full range of skills from all 4 phases.
