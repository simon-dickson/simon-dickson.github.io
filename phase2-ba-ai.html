What is this?
Phase 2 builds on your AI literacy with the practical skills to participate in AI projects. You'll learn how AI projects differ from traditional IT projects, how to write requirements for AI, and how to use no-code AI tools to prototype solutions.
Why it matters for BAs
AI projects fail when requirements are vague, data isn't ready, or stakeholders have unrealistic expectations. Business analysts who understand AI project lifecycles are the bridge that prevents these failures.
Duration & Approach
Approximately 3 months. Start with the AI project management course, then move to product management for AI. The no-code tools section is hands-on — build something small to solidify your learning.
🎯
Phase 2 Goal
Write an AI project requirements document for a real business problem. Include: problem statement, data requirements, success metrics, feasibility assessment, risk register, and stakeholder impact analysis. Build a simple no-code AI prototype to demonstrate the concept.
