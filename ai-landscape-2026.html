Model Comparison
Context Window tokens
The maximum amount of text a model can "see" at once — both your input and its output combined. A token is roughly ¾ of a word. A 200K token window fits about 150,000 words, or a full novel. Larger windows let models work with longer documents, codebases, or conversation histories without losing track of earlier content.
MMLU-Pro benchmark score
Massive Multitask Language Understanding — Professional. A standard benchmark testing a model's knowledge and reasoning across 57 academic subjects including law, medicine, maths, history and coding. Scores are expressed as a percentage of questions answered correctly. A score above 88% represents the current frontier of AI capability.

Key Trends 2026
06 trends
01 🤖
Agentic AI goes mainstream
LLMs are graduating from chatbots to autonomous agents capable of multi-step task execution. Agentic workflows now handle entire business processes with minimal human oversight.
02 🌏
China's open-source offensive
DeepSeek's disruptive release triggered a wave of Chinese open-weight models. Qwen, GLM, Kimi and others are gaining global adoption despite geopolitical tensions over data handling.
03 📐
Context windows explode
Llama 4 Scout's 10M token context window enables entire codebase analysis. Gemini 3 holds 1M+. Long context is shifting what's architecturally possible in AI applications.
04 ⚖️
Regulation tightens
The EU AI Act (effective Feb 2025) labels many LLM deployments as high-risk. US federal vs. state tensions intensify. Sovereign AI initiatives accelerate outside the US.
05 📱
On-device AI rises
Edge models from Mistral, Apple, Qualcomm and others run on laptops and phones without cloud connectivity. Privacy, latency and offline use cases drive adoption at the hardware layer.
06 🔬
AI-driven science
Google's AlphaEvolve demonstrated AI discovering new mathematical algorithms. 80% of initial healthcare diagnoses projected to involve AI analysis. Science acceleration is underway.
