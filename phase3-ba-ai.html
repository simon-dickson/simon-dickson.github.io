What is this?
Phase 3 focuses on AI ethics, governance, and regulation — the fastest-growing area of demand in AI projects. Organisations need people who can assess AI risk, ensure compliance, and build responsible AI practices. Your BA background in compliance and process makes this a natural fit.
Why this matters now
The EU AI Act is now being enforced, and organisations worldwide are scrambling to understand what it means for their AI systems. BAs who can conduct AI risk assessments and map compliance requirements are in extremely high demand.
Duration & Approach
Approximately 3 months. Start with the practical ethics course, then move to EU AI Act specifics, then finish with governance frameworks. This phase pairs well with seeking out real AI governance tasks at your organisation.
🎯
Phase 3 Goal
Create an AI risk assessment for a real or hypothetical AI system at your organisation. Classify it under the EU AI Act risk framework, identify bias and fairness concerns, define transparency requirements, and propose a governance structure with clear accountability and human oversight.
