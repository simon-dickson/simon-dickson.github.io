About Phase 2
Phase 2 transforms your data analyst intuition into structured machine learning engineering. You already understand data — now you'll learn to systematically build, evaluate and improve predictive models using the most important tools in the ML ecosystem.
Your Analyst Advantage
Your data analyst background is a major advantage here. EDA, business context, and knowing 'what good looks like' are things most ML beginners lack. Focus extra effort on the engineering side: Scikit-learn Pipelines, modular code, and proper train/test hygiene.
Duration & Pace
Approximately 3 months (Months 4–6 of the roadmap), building directly on the Python and software engineering foundations from Phase 1. Push your milestone project to GitHub as your first proper AI engineering portfolio piece.
🎯
Phase 2 Milestone Project
Build an end-to-end ML model on a Kaggle dataset from your industry. Cover: data ingestion → EDA → feature engineering → model selection → evaluation → report. Push to GitHub with full documentation. This becomes your first proper AI engineering portfolio piece.
💡
Study Tip
Don't just run models — focus on the engineering side: building Scikit-learn Pipelines, writing modular code, and proper train/test hygiene. Include: data ingestion, EDA, feature engineering, model selection, hyperparameter tuning, and a thorough evaluation report.
